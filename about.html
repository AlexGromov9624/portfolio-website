I’m a UX Designer and Front End Developer with seasoned experience in digital art, mobile development, and management. I enjoy taking complex problems and creating a usable, intuitive, and creative solution that provides a positive impact on user experience. I’ve always been fascinated when it comes to how software works under the hood, which lead me to learn coding where I strive to write legible and efficient code. Within the last decade, I’ve worked in Gaming, Television & Film, and the live events industry, specializing in 3D Design, Animation Techniques, Project Management and Client Relationship Management. Recently, I have transitioned into UX Design, continuing my journey to focusing on client empathy, design patterns, and creating memorable user experiences. When I’m not learning and burning the candle at both ends, I’m working on a long list of home projects. I love to work with my hands, so you’ll find me fixing something small to working on large home renovations. The whole process is very rewarding because it requires you to step outside of your comfort zone, conduct thorough research, create detailed drawings, and test out different ideas. Whether personal or business, I’m up for any challenge.
